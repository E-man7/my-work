Full-Stack Development & Mobile App Solutions

Front-End Development

I build intuitive, responsive, and engaging user interfaces using modern frameworks like React Native, React, and Vue.js. My front-end stack combines HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and modern design systems to deliver cross-platform applications that look sharp and run smoothly — from mobile apps for students to interactive dashboards for schools.

Mobile App Development

I specialize in cross-platform mobile apps using Expo, React Native, and real-time features powered by Supabase and Firebase. I create educational apps with features like GPS tracking for parents, teacher dashboards, and AI chatbots for students and instructors.

Back-End Development

My back-end expertise covers building secure, scalable APIs using Node.js, Express, and serverless solutions like Supabase Edge Functions. I design robust databases with PostgreSQL, Firebase Firestore, and Appwrite, ensuring data integrity and smooth performance — critical for education platforms and payment integrations.

DevOps & Deployment

I streamline deployments with CI/CD pipelines, Docker, and modern hosting solutions like AWS, Supabase Edge, and Netlify. I optimize infrastructure for scalability, so your apps stay reliable as user numbers grow.

Cybersecurity

With hands-on penetration testing experience for institutions, I ensure the software I build is secure by design — from secure authentication to payment gateways and local server hardening. I leverage industry best practices to protect data and maintain compliance.

UI/UX Design

I design clean, functional interfaces in Figma and Adobe XD, combining thoughtful UX with visually compelling layouts. From wireframes to clickable prototypes, I craft user journeys that prioritize clarity and engagement — especially for educational platforms.

WordPress & CMS Solutions

I develop and customize WordPress sites for businesses and nonprofits, integrating custom themes and plugins for easy content management, email listing, and lead generation.
